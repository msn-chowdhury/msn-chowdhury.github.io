About

Mohammad Samin Nur Chowdhury is a Ph.D. candidate in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Purdue University. His research focuses on developing innovative algorithms that combine physics-based modeling and machine learning for hyperspectral neutron computed tomography and multidimensional imaging applications.
